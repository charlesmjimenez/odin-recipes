Sinigang

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Small onion, chopped
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Plum Tomato
  • 4 cups water
  • Pork Chop
  • Chicken Broth Cube

Steps

  1. Heat vegeatable oil and add onion and garlic until softened
  2. Stir in ginger tomatos and pork chops
  3. Add water. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer untol pork is tender
  4. Add vegetables and the sinigang mix.
