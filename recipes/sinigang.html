Sinigang Recipe

Description

Sinigang is a Filipino sour soup made with tamarind, meat or seafood, and mixed vegetables. Its tangy flavor and comforting warmth make it a beloved staple in Filipino cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork (belly or ribs), cut into chunks
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 pack sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup string beans, cut
  • 1 cup water spinach (kangkong)
  • Fish sauce and salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pork in water until tender.
  2. Add onions and tomatoes; simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in radish, eggplant, and string beans; cook until tender.
  4. Add sinigang mix (or tamarind), season with fish sauce and salt.
  5. Add kangkong last; cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with rice.
